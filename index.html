Fun Facts About Space for Kids

The solar system consists of eight planets and their moons, as well as asteroids, comets, and other objects orbiting the Sun.

The Earth is the third planet in our Solar system and where we live on.

Mercury takes roughly three Earth months to orbit the Sun.

Mars may look warm but it's very cold.The whole of Mars is as cold as the South Pole.

There are no solid surfaces on Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, or Neptune, so you couldn't walk on them!

The Sun is a star that generates light and heat and is composed of heated gas.

Uranus and Neptune are almost similar in size (Uranus is the bigger one) and Neptune has just five rings and 14 moons, the most famous of which is Triton.

Asteroids are made mostly of rock with some composed of clay and different metals, mostly nickel and iron.
